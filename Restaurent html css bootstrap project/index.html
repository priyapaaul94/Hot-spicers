About Us

The Hot Spicers’s roots can be traced all the way back to 2020. Founded by Mrs.Shanmugapriya Manojunder the name Anandha Vilas Biriyani Hotel in Karur.
She always made a spicy Briyani forever, which over the years became synonymous with her brand and cooking styles, leading her to earn the nick name “HOT SPICERS” which would eventually became the name of our brand and restaurants. Hot Spicers has been delighting people from the rich to the poor for the last 3 years and the journey goes on!!!
